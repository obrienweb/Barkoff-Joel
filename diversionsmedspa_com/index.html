Medical Spa Albuquerque

Welcome to DiVersions Medical Spa where our only focus is a DiNamic New You! We are an exclusive Boutique Medical Spa that specializes in a very private and soothing environment to make you feel pampered and appreciated . By eliminating busy distractions, our only focus is on You and your beauty desires.
